### 🚀 About me

AI Agent & LLM application engineer who turns fuzzy ideas into working, trustworthy
agent products — the systems, retrieval, and evaluation that let them ship with
confidence. Recently at **Microsoft Research Asia**, I built an end-to-end AI
interview platform and a research knowledge-graph assistant. An **Economics (UCL)**
+ **Data Science (NUS)** background lets me bridge business judgment and hands-on
engineering.

### 🧠 Focus areas

- **Agent design & orchestration** — ReAct · Planner · MCP · multi-step tool use
- **Retrieval & knowledge systems** — hierarchical, traceable RAG & knowledge graphs
- **LLM evaluation** — LLM-as-Judge, rubric & trajectory-level quality assurance
